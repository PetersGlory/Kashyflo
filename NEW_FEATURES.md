Your spending at a glance!

### 2. **Smart Alerts** 🚨
Never be surprised by overspending again!

**Alert Types:**
- ⚠️ **Critical Alert** - You're spending way too much!
- 🟡 **Warning Alert** - Slow down, a category is over budget
- ✅ **Good News** - You're doing great, keep it up!

**Where You'll See Them:**
Alerts appear on your main dashboard, right at the top where you can't miss them.

**Examples:**
- "You've exceeded your Food budget by ₦5,000"
- "You have only ₦8,000 (15%) remaining - budget carefully!"
- "Critical: You are significantly overspending. Slow down!"

**Why It Works:**
Real-time alerts mean you catch problems before they get worse. The app does the math so you don't have to!

### 3. **Weekly Summary** 📈
See if this week is better than last week!

**What It Shows:**
- **This Week's Total Spending** - How much you've spent this week

- **Trend Indicator** - Is spending going up or down?
- **Encouragement** - "Good trend!" when you're improving

**Example:**
```
This Week: ₦125,000
Last Week: ₦145,000
↓ 13.8% improvement - Good trend!
```

**Why It's Helpful:**
You can quickly see if you're improving your spending habits week-over-week.

## How to Use New Features

### View Your Analytics:
1. Open Kashyflo
2. Tap the 📊 chart icon in the header
3. Scroll through your insights:
   - Budget health score
   - Spending overview
   - Trend chart (7 days)
   - Category breakdown pie chart
   - Detailed category list

### Check Your Alerts:
1. Open Dashboard
2. Look at the top of the screen for colored alert cards
3. Read the message and take action
4. If you see "Good trend!" - celebrate! 🎉

### Add Expense with New Selector:
1. Tap the **+** button
2. Tap a **colored category button**
3. Enter the amount
4. Tap Save

### Review Weekly Progress:
1. On Dashboard, look for "This Week's Spending" card
2. Compare with last week's amount
3. See if trend is improving (↓ good) or increasing (↑ watch out)

## FAQ

**Q: Why am I getting a "Critical Budget Alert"?**
A: You're spending significantly more than expected for this point in the month. Consider pausing non-essential spending.

**Q: What does the budget health score mean?**
A: It's a 0-100 score based on your spending pace. Higher is better. 100 means perfect budget adherence.

**Q: Can I change category colors?**
A: Currently, colors are preset. We're working on customization for future versions!

**Q: Why is my pie chart empty?**
A: You haven't added any expenses yet this month. Add a few expenses and come back!

**Q: Does the trend chart only show 7 days?**
A: Yes, the main dashboard shows the last 7 days. Full monthly trends are coming soon!

**Q: What's the difference between "Budget" and "Remaining"?**
A: **Budget** = How much you allocated for the category
**Remaining** = How much you have left to spend
