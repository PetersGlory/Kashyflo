# Kashyflo Setup Guide

Welcome to Kashyflo! This guide will help you get started with your smart budgeting app.

## Getting Started (First Time Users)

### Step 1: Access the App
- Open the app on your browser
- Add to home screen if on mobile (tap ⋮ → "Install app")

### Step 2: Set Your Monthly Salary
1. Click the **Settings** icon (⚙️) in the top right
2. Enter your monthly salary amount in Naira
3. Review the default budget allocations:
   - Rent: 20%
   - Food: 30%
   - Savings: 15%
   - Buffer: 10%
   - Entertainment: 10%
   - Transport: 10%
   - Other: 5%

### Step 3: Customize Your Budget (Optional)
If you want to adjust how your salary is split:
1. Change the percentage for each category
2. Make sure the total equals 100%
3. Click "Save Allocations"

The percentages should automatically update to keep the total at 100%.

### Step 4: Add Your First Expense
1. Return to the Dashboard by clicking the Kashyflo logo
2. Click the **+** button (Floating Action Button) at the bottom right
3. Select a category
4. Enter the amount
5. Add a description (optional, e.g., "Lunch at work")
6. Click "Save Expense"

The dashboard will immediately update to show your remaining budget.

## Understanding Your Dashboard

### Budget Card (Top)
Shows your total monthly salary and days until next salary payment.

### Category Cards
Each category shows:
- **Category Name** and allocated percentage
- **Spent Amount**: How much you've spent
- **Progress Bar**: Visual representation of spending
- **Remaining Amount**: How much you can still spend
- **Status Badge**: Shows percentage spent

### Color Indicators
- 🟢 **Green**: More than 50% remaining (you're good!)
- 🟡 **Yellow**: 20-50% remaining (be cautious)
- 🔴 **Red**: Less than 20% remaining (limit spending)

## Troubleshooting

### "No salary added yet"
**Solution**: Go to Settings and add your monthly salary amount first.

### "Allocations don't add up to 100%"
**Solution**: Adjust the percentages so they total exactly 100%. The save button will be disabled until they do.

### Expense not saving
**Solution**: 
1. Make sure you've selected a category (it should be highlighted)
2. Make sure the amount is entered correctly
3. Check your internet connection (though app works offline)

### Lost my data
**Solution**: 
- Data is stored locally on your device
- Clearing browser cache/data will delete it
- Back up important data regularly
- Never clear browser data without backing up first

### App not working offline
**Solution**:
1. First visit must be online to cache the app
2. Subsequent visits work fully offline
3. Clear your browser cache and reload once while online
4. The Service Worker will cache everything

## FAQ

**Q: Is my data backed up?**
A: Data is stored on your device only. Consider exporting regularly if needed.

**Q: Can I use Kashyflo on multiple devices?**
A: Currently, data is local to each device. Multi-device sync is on the roadmap.

**Q: What if I accidentally delete an entry?**
A: Go to History page and click the trash icon to delete. Once deleted, it can't be recovered.

**Q: Can I change categories?**
A: Currently, categories are fixed. You can modify their percentages and descriptions.

**Q: Does Kashyflo track savings automatically?**
A: The app tracks how much you've allocated to Savings. You need to manage actual transfers yourself.

**Q: Is there a way to export my data?**
A: Currently not built-in, but data is stored in browser's IndexedDB and can be exported manually.
